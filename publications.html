Particle Filter-Based Localization Using Visual Feature Synchronization in GNSS-Denied Navigation
A. Sanlan, H. T. Bagci, E. Koyuncu
2025 Integrated Communications, Navigation and Surveillance Conference (ICNS), Brussels, Belgium, pp. 1–8
DOI IEEE
Abstract

This paper presents a particle filter-based localization approach utilizing visual feature synchronization for navigation in GNSS-denied environments. The method leverages visual correspondences between onboard camera imagery and geo-referenced maps to maintain accurate position estimates without satellite-based signals.
